Our Services

Our services are designed to support individuals seeking a personalized and integrative approach to wellness. Care is collaborative, flexible, and tailored to each patient's needs.

Holistic Health Consultation

A comprehensive intake that explores health history, current concerns, lifestyle patterns, and wellness goals. This consultation forms the foundation for individualized recommendations and ongoing care.

Custom Herbal & Natural Formulas

The foundation of Quantum Natural Medicine is the creation of custom herbal formulas. Through an in-person or phone consultation, Dr. Vokshori conducts a detailed assessment that may include discussion of symptoms, lifestyle, health history, and energetic patterns.

Based on this evaluation, herbs are carefully selected, adjusted, and combined to reflect each patient's unique needs. Formulas may be refined over time as the body responds and health goals evolve.

Lifestyle & Wellness Guidance

Supportive guidance related to daily habits, nutrition, stress balance, sleep, and overall wellbeing. Recommendations are realistic and designed to integrate into everyday life.

Integrative Ongoing Support

Follow-up care allows formulas and recommendations to be adjusted as progress is made and needs change.
