About Dr. Pari Vokshori

Dr. Pari Vokshori

Natural Medicine Practitioner

Dr. Pari Vokshori is a natural medicine practitioner based in Los Angeles, California. Since the mid-1990s, she has worked with a wide variety of patients presenting with diverse health concerns, taking a personalized and integrative approach to care.

Beginning in 1994, Dr. Vokshori worked closely with Matt van Benschoten and gained extensive hands-on experience treating patients with a broad range of conditions and wellness goals. This early clinical experience helped shape her practical, patient-centered approach and deepened her understanding of individualized natural care.

Her work focuses on understanding the full picture of each patient, including health history, lifestyle patterns, stress, and long-term trends, rather than addressing symptoms in isolation. Care plans are designed to be thoughtful, adaptable, and supportive, evolving as the patient's needs change over time.

Our Philosophy

At Quantum Natural Medicine, health is viewed as a dynamic and individualized process. The goal is not quick fixes, but careful assessment, personalized guidance, and long-term support that helps patients move toward greater balance and wellbeing.
