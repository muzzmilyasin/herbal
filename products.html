Custom Herbal Formula

The foundation of Quantum Natural Medicine is the creation of custom herbal formulas tailored to each individual. Through an in-person or phone consultation, Dr. Pari Vokshori conducts a detailed assessment that may include discussion of health history, lifestyle factors, and energetic patterns.

Based on this evaluation, herbs are selected and customized to reflect the individual rather than relying solely on pre-made combinations. This personalized approach allows formulas to be adjusted over time as needs and goals change, providing a higher level of precision and individualized care.
